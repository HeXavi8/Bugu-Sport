Transforming Campus Sports

Bugu Sport (不咕约球) is more than just an app—it's a movement to revolutionize how students engage with sports on campus. Our WeChat Mini Program connects athletes, organizes competitions, and builds lasting sports communities across universities.

Ready to join the community? Download WeChat and scan the QR code to experience the future of campus sports. From pickup games to organized tournaments, from team formation to skill development—everything you need is just a scan away.

Why Choose Bugu Sport?

Built by students, for students—with features that actually matter

  • Smart Match Making

    Our intelligent system connects players based on skill level, location, and availability. Experience the thrill of Football Manager in real life—organize tournaments, manage teams, and track statistics like never before.

  • Professional Integration

    Bridge the gap between amateur and professional sports. We're building partnerships with coaches, scouts, and referees to bring professional standards to campus athletics and create new opportunities for student athletes.

  • Multi-Campus Network

    Connect across 29 campuses in our growing network. Whether you're a student or faculty member, expand your sports circle, find training partners, and participate in inter-campus competitions.

Organic Growth Story

Since our launch in November 2020, Bugu Sport has grown entirely through word-of-mouth and user satisfaction. Without any paid advertising, we've built a thriving community of students and faculty who believe in the power of sports to connect and inspire.

"Our success isn't measured just in downloads, but in the friendships formed, the games played, and the communities built. Every user represents a story of connection through sports."
— The Bugu Sport Team

We continue to iterate and improve, always listening to our community and adapting to serve the evolving needs of campus sports. Our platform isn't just growing—it's evolving with purpose.
