Project Overview

Bugu Sports is a WeChat mini program focused on campus sports, primarily targeting students, including college, primary, and secondary school students.

Development Purpose

  • Provide a platform for niche sports fans to connect with like-minded individuals.
  • Enhance sports communication channels between schools by offering more opportunities for students from different schools and campuses to interact directly, thereby expanding their sports networks and opportunities for exchange and collaboration.
  • Organize annual inter-campus sports competitions and create “sports festivals” for different schools, similar to the annual Oxford-Cambridge Boat Race and the Harvard-Yale Football Game.
  • Improve the atmosphere and impact of campus sports activities by developing campus sports culture, allowing students to enjoy a more fulfilling sports life on campus.
  • Enhance the professionalism of campus sports by integrating professional resources into campuses and exploring opportunities to broaden the range of professional sports offerings. (Inspired by the development of campus basketball in the U.S. and campus football in Japan.)
  • Explore the commercial potential of campus sports by learning from established student sports leagues like the NCAA.

Platform Support

Match: Invitations, Game Discussions, Inter-School Communication

  • Users can select their preferred sports and filter other users based on their school and campus to find individuals with similar interests.
  • Additionally, users can apply as individuals or teams to compete against other users or teams who are interested in competing.

Street Game: Search for Pitches, Find Partners, Accessible to Teachers and Students

  • We have compiled information on sports pitches for ten schools and 29 campuses.
  • Users can select a pitch at any specific school or campus, choose a time they wish to play, and either start or join a pick-up game.

Team: Find Teams, Join a Team, Manage Teams

  • Users can create and manage their own teams.
  • Search for teams they are interested in and apply to join.

Authentication: Event Authentication, Team Authentication, Personal Authentication

  • Users can gain the rights to create events or teams after completing identity authentication.

Events: Find Events, Join Events, Release Information

  • Event organizers can manage participants and upload detailed information, progress updates, and event data.
  • Users can search for events they are interested in and join them as individuals or teams.

Personal: Private Messages, Verify Invitations, Personal Page

  • Users can reply to received private messages and verify invitations for events or teams.
  • View and customize their personal homepage.
