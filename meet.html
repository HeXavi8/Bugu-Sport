Partnerships and Professional Resources

  • We aim to collaborate with more schools' sports departments and associations to foster a stronger campus sports community and provide users with enhanced services.
  • We also plan to introduce professional resources, such as coaches and referees, to offer expert guidance to students and establish a scouting system to identify and nurture potential athletes.

Some Thoughts

  • Our team consists of four software engineers and one UI/UX designer. As sports enthusiasts, we focus on developing campus sports. We strive to enhance the campus sports atmosphere and provide students with more opportunities to compete with peers from different campuses and schools.
  • Developing this mini-program serves to explore the possibilities of campus sports and gain practical insights, which will help us support the future growth of campus sports.
  • Given our initial experience and relatively small team size, Bugu-Sports still has various areas for improvement and requires ongoing iteration.
  • Through our exploration of on-campus sports, we've encountered many challenges and resistance. Nevertheless, our commitment to advancing campus sports remains strong. We sincerely hope that more people will engage with and contribute to campus sports, enabling us to create new opportunities for their development together.
