Development Purpose

What We Want To Do

For Students and Teachers

  • Provide a platform for niche sports fans to connect with like-minded individuals.
  • Enhance sports communication channels between schools by offering more opportunities for students from different schools and campuses to interact directly, thereby expanding their sports social circles and avenues for exchange and cooperation.

For Campuses

  • Organize annual inter-campus sports competitions and create “sports festivals” for different schools, similar to the annual Oxford-Cambridge Boat Race and the Harvard-Yale Football Game.
  • Improve the atmosphere and impact of campus sports activities by developing campus sports culture, allowing students to enjoy a more fulfilling sports life on campus.

More

  • Enhance the professionalism of campus sports by integrating professional resources into campuses and exploring opportunities to broaden the range of professional sports offerings. (Inspired by the development of campus basketball in the U.S. and campus football in Japan.)
  • Explore the commercial potential of campus sports by learning from established student sports leagues like the NCAA.

Feedback

If you have any suggestions and ideas, you would like to share them with us. We are more than happy to receive your Email.
